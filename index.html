94th EWG on MCDA 2022

September 15-17, 2022


Elounda Town, Agios Nikolaos, Crete, Greece

Multiple Criteria Decision Aiding and Sustainable Development Goals

It is our great pleasure to invite you to the 94th meeting of the EURO Working Group on Multiple Criteria Decision Aiding (EWG-MCDA 94). The EWG-MCDA 94 is organized by the Department of Management Science and Technology, Hellenic Mediterranean University, and the School of Production Engineering and Management, Technical University of Crete. It will take place at Elounda Town, in Agios Nikolaos Crete, Greece, September 15-17, 2022

Elounda town is located 10km north of Agios Nikolaos, near the northwest side of the bay of Mirabello.

Main conference topic

Environmental decisions are often complex and multifaceted and involve many different stakeholders with different priorities or objectives. Multicriteria decision analysis (MCDA) tools can be applied to assess value judgments of individual decision makers or multiple stakeholders regarding the environmental risk management. The 94th meeting of the EURO working group on Multiple Criteria Decision Aiding (EWG-MCDA 94) aims to pinpoint the potential of multiple criteria decision aiding methods as an ideal support methodology for the environmental risk management.

Indicative Topics

  • Decision support toolbox for environmental risk management

  • Environmental, economic and social impacts of low carbon technology options

  • Risks and Uncertainties of Climate Change Mitigation Portfolios

  • Multi-criteria consideration of risk and uncertainty for climate and energy policy

  • Portfolio Analysis in Energy and Climate Policy

  • MCDA and tools evaluating alternative energy pathways

  • Quantifying Environmental impact and MCDA methods

  • Benefits of Flexible Policy Implementation

Submission

We cordially invite everyone interested in multiple criteria decision aiding to submit abstracts for the meeting. The abstract can entail new methodological approaches and concepts, real application case studies, or other ways to promote MCDA as the attractive field within the OR society. The works related to the topic of the meeting are encouraged but not restricted.

There are two types of submission available:

Regular communication

These abstracts will be presented as part of the final program. Authors will have a 20-minute presentation to present their research to the audience.

Discussion paper

Abstract for the authors who wish to participate with a submitted abstract but do not require to present during the meeting.

Submission form

Abstracts must be written on a template available HERE.

The abstracts should not be longer than one page. Please select desired type of submission.

Please send your submission via email to kostas@dpem.tuc.gr

Key dates and deadlines

  • Abstract submission deadline: July 31, 2022
  • Notification of acceptance to authors: August 14, 2022, at the latest
  • Registration to the meeting deadline: August 30, 2022
  • Event dates: September 15-17, 2022
